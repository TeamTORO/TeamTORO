Adopt
Woah, thanks for being interested in adopting!
Adopting goes straight to our partners, that means they handle the background check before the adoption. Just state the name of the cat or dog you want to adopt and that you came from us!

Here are MARO's requirements before adopting:

- the rescues are free but not the vaccinations and we ask to participate in the expenses for spay/neuter which is usually all done already and saves you a lot of efforts

- Php 1000 includes anti rabies, canine 5in1 resp. feline 3in1 vaccine incl. booster shot. A puppy needs 4 shots which cost Php 350 up at a vet clinic alone!

- we got lots of questions about your place and setup. It must be suitable for our understanding. We prefer responsible adopters who are willing and can afford to bring a rescue to the vet if needed!

- to own an animal must be thought over and landlord and family must agree! One needs to be aware it will generate expenses and efforts

- we may do a home visit and to start with you should visit the rescue to see if it accepts you and if the rescue is what you expected from a single photo

- it's not an instant process. We made bad experiences before and had to take back rescues or were returned. We try to avoid this. We put a lot of efforts into their survival. Thanks for understanding! Pease be patient when you apply and remind us occassionally thanks!
