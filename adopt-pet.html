Our partnered organizations have pets available for adoption, this means that one of them could be part of your family. How cool is that!?

Choose from our partnered organizations!

Mayari Animal Rescue Organization (MARO)

Their main focus is on providing treatment and recovery as appropriate to those street dogs and cats who are sick, malnourished or otherwise desperately in need of care. they provide help for the suffering animals right on the spot and within their familiar environment as much as we relocate worse cases to our shelter for intensive care.

Wild Rescue Crew

Run by a great man named Imanuel Theodor Willburger-Wild who rescued more than 600 puppies and counting! He goes to different malls in Cebu, Philippines to perform just to have an opportunity for people to adopt his puppies. He performed over 100 shows in different malls for his advocacy!
